Quick Launch Buttons

The Image Studio Quick Launch buttons are located on the ribbon task bar above the tabs. These buttons are accessible from all the tabbed ribbon views. They include Save Analysis, Undo, Redo, Start Scan, Analysis Type Print, Show Single Image, Show Multiple Images, LI-COR Biotech News Feed, Zoom In, and Zoom Out.
